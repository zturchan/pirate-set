###.## Bribe

701.99 Mint
	701.99a To "Mint N" means put a colorless artifact token named Gold onto the battlefield. It has “Sacrifice this artifact: Add one mana of any color to your mana pool."

702.999. Letter of Marque
	702.999a Letter of Marque is a triggered ability. “Letter of Marque” on a permanent means "Whenever you cast a creature that shares a color with this card you may pay [cost] and exile this card from your hand backing that creature. When that creature dies return this card to your hand"

	702.999b Cards that are in the exile zone as the result of a Letter of Marque ability “Back” the creature targeted
by that ability. The phrase “backed creature” refers to the object targeted by the Letter of Marque ability,
regardless of whether or not that object is still a creature.

	702.999c The card with Letter of Marque continues to back the chosen creature as long as the card with
Letter of Marque remains exiled and the creature remains on the battlefield. The card continues to back
that object even if it changes controller or stops being a creature, as long as it remains on the
battlefield.
